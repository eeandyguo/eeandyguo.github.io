Yuanfang Guo (Andy)
郭园方


Ph.D. & Assistant Professor

I received my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Hong Kong, in 2009 and 2015, respectively. I am currently an Assistant Professor with the State Key Laboratory of Information Security, Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. My research interests include image/video watermarking, data hiding, forensics, compression, restoration, and enhancement.
